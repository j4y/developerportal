Indicates that the face detector has started tracking a new face.

When the face tracker detects a face for the first time method is called. The receiver should expect that tracking continues until detection has stopped.

Indicates that the face detector has stopped tracking a face.

When the face tracker no longer finds a face this method is called. The receiver should expect that there is no face tracking until the detector is started.
